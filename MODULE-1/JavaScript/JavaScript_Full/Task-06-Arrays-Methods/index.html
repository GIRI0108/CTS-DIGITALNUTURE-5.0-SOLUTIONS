Community Bulletin

The city council is organizing many useful events for residents this month. The upcoming programs include tree planting, health camp, blood donation, water conservation awareness and yoga sessions. These events are planned to bring people together and improve community participation. Residents can register through the portal and check event details before visiting. Volunteers are also welcome to help with event arrangements, registration support and public guidance. The portal will be updated regularly with event timings, locations and important announcements. Residents are encouraged to check the portal often and take part in local activities.
