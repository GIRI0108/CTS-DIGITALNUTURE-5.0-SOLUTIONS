Community Bulletin

City Council Community Update

The Local Community Event Portal helps residents stay connected with upcoming events, awareness programs, workshops, health camps, and cultural activities. Residents can view event details, check available seats, register online, and receive useful information about community services.

This month, the city council is focusing on environment awareness, health support, digital learning, and youth participation. Citizens are encouraged to take part in local events and support community development programs.

Volunteers can also participate in tree planting, blood donation, food drives, and educational workshops. These programs help improve public engagement and create a stronger connection between residents and the city council.
